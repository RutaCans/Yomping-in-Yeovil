About Us

Welcome to Yomping in Yeovil — your gateway to exploring the green heart of our town and surrounding areas. Our guided walks take you through Yeovil's most beautiful natural spaces, following woodland trails, freshwater streams, and the peaceful bends of the River Yeo. Just a short drive from Yeovil, you can explore historical landmarks, stunning landscapes, and welcoming communities. Whether you're looking for a relaxing stroll, a family‑friendly outing, or an energising adventure outdoors, there's always something new to discover.

Our tours regularly explore some of Yeovil's much‑loved parks and green escapes, including Nine Springs Park, Yeovil Country Park, Riverside Walk, Ham Hill Country Park, Tintinhull gardens and Staple Hill Forest to name few. These spaces are often praised for their calm atmosphere, clean paths, wildlife, and welcoming community feel — making them perfect places to unwind and enjoy nature.

Come and join us! Sign up today to meet friendly people, discover new walking routes, and enjoy the stunning landscapes Yeovil has to offer.
